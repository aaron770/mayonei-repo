How can we honour you and your loved ones?

Furthermore, at Mayanot Israel, we want to share the merit of our Torah study by directly honoring you and your loved ones. Whether on the yahrzeit of a parent who passed away, the birthday of a beloved child or grandchild or any special occasion such as a Bat/Barmitzvah, we would like to dedicate a day to learning in their name or good deeds done in their honour. honoring a loved one through charity and Torah study not only gives spiritual meaning to the day but also gives lasting merit to your loved one forever. You will receive a video of the event as well as a card commemorating the day..

About our Kollel

Mayanot Israel is a Jewish Torah learning centre (Kollel) located in the center of Israel within the holy city of Bnei Brak; a city renowned for it’s high level of Torah study and scholarship. Within Bnei Brak and Israel, Mayanot Israel is unique because the centre invites and welcomes Jews from every group from ultra-orthodox Chasidim to the secular to gather together and unite as one people to study Torah. In effect, Mayanot Israel is part of a larger movement and transformation taking place all over Israel. At our Kollel in Bnei Brak, on a daily basis, 45 married men from all walks of life come to study full or part time Jewish Law, mysticism and the sacred teachings of the Ba’al Shem Tov (Master of the Good name).Every month, there are festive gatherings (Farbrengens) of over 200 people who come to hear words of Torah. Mayanot Israel also helps to develop these young students into future Jewish leaders by organizing charitable work in the community.
